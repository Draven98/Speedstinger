Meet SpeedStinger!

SpeedStinger is my 2015 Vex Skyrise Robot. He won 3rd place in the Skills USA New York Conference. He gets his name from being the fastest robot there. His drivetrain was carefully put together and altered using specific gear rations.







The arm extends using two joints allowing for a higher lift and more lift control. There are two ways to pick up cubes. You can hook the arm inside or open the claw. Average delivery time on the field for one cube is 5 seconds. Stacking is achievable.
